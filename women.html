Women's Wing

The Women's Wing is a body of the church that is made up of women over the age of 21 years.

The Women's Wing is responsible for:


(i) Developing and strenthening the spiritual life of women of the church.
(ii) Plan programmes which will meet the needs of the younger and older women, wives and mothers.
(iii) Ensure that women are of service in branches and the local community.
(iv) The development of leadership amongst church women.
(v) Seeking information and developing understanding between all women of Revelation Blessed Peace Church and women of other donominations.

Bodies that make the Women's Wing

Women's Wing Executive Council

The women's wing Executive Coucil is the mother body of all bodies that make up the women's wing. The core mandates of the women's wing are:
-to act as a consultative body in all matters affecting Revelation Blessed Peace Church women activities.
- to receive reports from regional committees and present an annual report to the conference.

Women's wing regional committees

The women's regional committees are responsible for taking care of women's affairs on their respective regions. Their core mandate is:
-to insure that the women's wing core mandates are being carried out at regional level.
-to help solve issues that affect women in their region
-to offer advice and clarity about issues where necessary.

Women's wing branch committee

The women's wing branch committees take care of the day to day issues that affect women in their branches. They are responsible for:
-making sure women attend church services
-equiping women with life skills necessary for a good living
-preach and teach the word of God to other women
-pray for the congregation

Revelation Blessed Peace Church

Revelation Blessed Peace Church was founded by the late Archbishop Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba. A citizen of Botswana, a man of God. With the calling of God, Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba then founded the now Revelation Blessed Peace Church in March 1976.

During his time as a young man Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba worked as a miner digging for gold in South Africa. It was then as he aged that the calling of the Lord came to him. Before officially founding and registering Revelation Blessed Peace Church of Botswana. Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba had a congregation which he offered prayed to the Lord with. It is with these congregation that Revelation Blessed Peace Church would be founded.

As he and his congregation offered prayer unto the Lord, Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba and his people went and joined the now established Spiritual Healing Church. Headed by then the late Prophet Jacob Mokaleng Motswasele. These two men were blessed with the ability to prophesy, and it was decided that Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba would be the direct assistant of the Prophet Jacob Mokaleng Motswasele of Spiritual Healing Church. Administaring healing to people and relaying words of prophesy to the people. A great relationship was then forged and the two prophets administered towards God and His people.

It was then that now God would call Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba to form his own church. As rightfully so, Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba and his people would now leave Spiritual Healing Church of Prophet Jacob Mokaleng Motswasele. It was now in the year 1976 that Revelation Blessed Peace Church would be officially registered. At the time it was registered Revelation Blessed Peace Church had more than 2000 members under the leadership of Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba.

The church continued offering prayer and healing the sick. Peoples lives were changed. But sadly in the year 1986 the then founder of Revelation Blessed peace Church was laid to rest. After his death a new leader was put in place to lead the church. The now Archbishop of Revelation Blessed Peace Church, Archbishop T.M Kepaletswe. Whom under his leadership the church has grown to now having 53 branches in 5 regions of Botswana.

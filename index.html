A Few Words About Us

Origins

Founded in March 1976 by the late Prophet Matlho Kepaletswe Dichaba. Revelation Blessed Peace Church came into being. Healing, comforting and loving all people according to the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Youth

As God has trusted the youth, Revelation Blessed Peace Church too have youth that are changing lives of others by preaching, teaching and sharing the word of God. With singing and playing instruments we praise God.

Women

Women as mothers in church and outside. Usher us with their comforting words into the righteous path. With love and gentlenes our ways to the Lord are made perfect. Blessed are those who follow Christ.

Features that Make us Unique

Isaiah 44:22 - I have blotted out, like a thick cloud, your transgretions, and like a cloud, your sins. Return to Me, for I have redeemed you.

Donations

Revelation Blessed Peace Church offers support to the less privilaged by means of donations thus encouraging the spirit of giving through voluntarism.

Weddings

The church has marriage officers who are appointed by Ministry of Labour and Home Affairs through the marriage act. Anyone wishing to be joined in the holy matrimony are welcomed by the church.

Funerals

We have pastors/reverends/senior pastors who conducts funeral proceedings to everyone, irrespective of their religious affiliation thus encouraging the spirit of togethernes.

Church Service

Our chuch service are conducted every sunday from 11:00hrs to 13:00hrs. We also have prayer and support on Wednesdays and Fridays to encourage members to learn the bible and be prayerful.

Prophecy & Healing

We have men and women who are gifted in the spirit of prophecy...check prophecy in the internet

Brass Band

Our brass band started in 2013 to encourage youth to love, sing, participate in the church and also to attract new members especially youth.

I'm New

Welcome to Revelation Blessed Peace Church, a place where everyone is welcome.

What to expect

A spiritual, warm, loving and an accepting environment. You will sing, pray, learn the bible and have fun. You will experience inner peace that fulfills the heart. You will be met with smiles that will brighten your day.

Find us

Our headquaters is in Kgagodi, Botswana. We also have 52 branches across Botswana and one in Mafikeng, South Africa. Click here if you would like to visit. We will help you find a branch near you.

Dress code

When visiting us you should put on long slived shirts with a coat. Long trousers for men and skirts that go beyond the knee level for women. Women also should put on a hat or a head scarf to cover their hair.

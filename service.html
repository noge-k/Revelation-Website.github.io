Church Services / Tirelo ya kereke

Below are scheduled times of our church services

Sunday Service

Our Sunday service starts at 11am and ends at 1pm. It is in this service that we sing, prayer and speak the word of the Lord. After the church service there are other church activities that take place for example choir practice, further discussions about the word preached, visits to the sick and those who have lost loved ones.

Wednesday Service

The Wednesday service starts at 6pm and ends at 8pm. The service is mainly dedicated to bible study and choir practice. It is in this service that we learn further the word of the Lord, it's interpretation, we learn how to pray and sing.

All night prayer service

All night prayers are church services when we get to pray, sing and preach the word through out the night. All night prayers are scheduled of when they will be. In our all night prayer service, everyone and antone is given the right and freedom to teach the word of God. Usually before teachings can begin a bible verse is read out to the people and everyone who is available can preach/teach others life lessons relating to what the bible verse is talking about.

Features that nake our services unique

We are people who extremely in love with God and His son Jesus Christ.

Bible teachings

In Revelation Blessed Peace Church. People are taught the word of God by preachers, decons, evangelists and reverends. In every church service the bible is used to uplift, encourage and inspire people to live better lives. The teaching of the word of God is not only for people with desginations. The general congress too can teach the word of God. Every Sunday service a schedule is read to notify people who will teach the word of God when it is time for preaching.

Singing worship songs

As God is worshiped in different ways. In Revelation Blessed Peace Church we sing songs that praise and worship our Lord Jesus Christ. Songs are sang by the whole congregation. This allows people to worship and praise their Lord through song and dance. We also have church choirs whom also take part in worshiping the Lord.

Drinking holy water

During our services there is a time when the whole congregation lines up to drink holy water. The water is prayed for by Pastors in the name of Jesus Christ. Then people come one at a time to have a drink of the holy water. People have been healed through drinking the holy water. The water also revives peoples souls and helps cleans their bodies.

Prayer for people

Reverends/pastors pray for people after the word of the Lord has been preached to people. Everyone who has attended the service is prayed for. Every problem, afflicition and troubles are prayed for by the pastors, asking God to intervine and help eliviate people from their troubles. School kids, teachers, working people, business owners, those seeking any other blessing too are prayed for.

Church updates

After the preaching, teaching and prayer for people is done. The church secretaries update the congregation on plans and upcoming events of the church so as to imform people for them to prepare for the next coming service and events.

New member recognition

It is in our church that every new member who has visited the church is acknowledged. New visitors say their names, where they are from and state their case of why they have visited the church. After that all the congregation claps hands for them as a sign of welcoming in the church.

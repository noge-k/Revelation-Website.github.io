Youth Wing

The Youth Wing is a body of members of the church below the age of 40.

The Youth Wing is responsible for:


(i) Doing youth-directed ministry.
(ii) Being the youth ministry organisation of the church.
(iii) Ministry with youth, for youth.
(iv) Developing lay leadership among youth.
(v) Calling youth to be followers of Jesus.
(vi) Advocacy for the concerns and agendas of youth.
(vii) Channeling youth presence and youth voices throughout the church.
(viii) Promoting youth-selected program priorities, which have recently included vocation, spiritual discernment, racism and at-risk youth. (ix) Co-operating and supporting emerging youth ministry initiatives with the church.
(x) Maintaining communication and networks throughout the church with area/district, annual and jurisdictional conference for youth ministry.

Bodies that make the Youth Wing

Sunday School / Sekolo sa bana sa tshipi

The Sunday school is a sector under the youth win. It's core mandate is to groom children into the character of Jesus Christ. Children are taught the values of love, respect, togethernes and cooperation. It is in Sunday school that many of our youth get their first encounter with the wisdom and knowledge about Jesus Christ and what it means to be a christian. Our Sunday school lessons are taught by youth and elders of the church teaching and cherishing children to become responsible christians and civilians in their respective regions. If you have an interest in joining or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.

Church Choir / Baopedi

Revelation Blessed Peace Church is blessed with choirs that sing praises to God. It is in these choirs that many of the youth are found at. Choirs encourage a sense of togetherness that is a sense of team work in working towards a common goal. In Revelation Blessed Peace Church everyone is allowed to join the choir there is no age restriction. Children as little as 5 years can join the choir even elders as old as 60 years can join. The choir also plays a role in building people spiritually as songs sang to praise and worship God helps build a strong relationship between people and God. If you have an interest in joining or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.

Newsletter / Lebega dikgang

The youth wing is also built of the Newsletter team. Their core mandate is to cover church events via videos, written word and images. These are the journalists of the church covering every major even of the church. The Newsletter is made up mostly of youth, it is in these organisation that youth get to learn basic reporting skills. Youth are taught how to take videos and shoot good quality images that will be published in the annual newsletter magazine that reports on the churches annual events that took place. it is also in these organisation that a sense of belong is forged among youth. Anyone can join the newsletter team even if they are not youth. If you have an interest in joining or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.

Brass Band / Baletsi ba diphala

The Brass Band is another important sector in the youth wing. It is made up of mostly youth that play brass instruments and drums. the brass band's main mandate is to help build people spiritualy as they play songs of praise and worship to the people of God. it is in the brass band that youth are taught the importance of prayer, caring for others and being involved in church activities. Youth that make the brass band are seen as examplary towards upcoming youngsters because in all church events they are there lending a helping hand where needed. They pray for the sick, wounded and those who are celebrating. Their praise is said to attract the holy spirit where ever they go. Everyoe is wlecome to join the brass band. No one is restricted even if they are not youth. If you have an interest in joining or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.

Youth Committees / Makgotla a banana

The youth wing is made up of youth committees. It is thses committees that run the youth ministry in our church. The Youth Executive Council is the mother body which governs all youth committees. After the Youth Exexcutive Council, the youth wing has the Youth Regional Committees that run the youth ministry at regional levels. Then finaly the youth is made up of Youth Branch Commiteees that run the day to day activities of the youth ministries at branch level. It is the combination of all these committees that run the procceedings of the youth directed ministry in Revelation Blessed Peace Church. If you have an interest in joining or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.

Youth / Banana

As the biggest part in the youth ministry. The youth play a vital role in the proceedings of the church. It is the youth that run the Sunday school, Brass band, Newsletter team, church choir,and youth committees. Nation wide the youth play a vital role in the growth of the church. The youth are given great responsibility to groom them into tomorrows leaders. We as the church are proud of our youth in every aspect that the church requires the youth to take part they are always there, giving their all for the ministry of Jesus Christ's work to be done. As the church we offer a helping hand towards growing the youth by being there with them when running projects, offering words of encouragement and giving advice where necessary. If you have an interest in joining the youth or landing a hand please contact us and we will assit you according to your request.
